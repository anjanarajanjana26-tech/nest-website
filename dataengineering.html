Data Engineering

The field of data engineering is concerned with designing, constructing, and maintaining the support systems and infrastructures necessary for data inbound, storage, processing, and analysis. Data engineers manage huge sets of data in a real-time environment. They are supposed to provide high quality of information that is really usable by different business departments. Data engineers deal with large volumes of data, often in real-time, and their role is crucial in enabling businesses to extract valuable insights from their data assets. They work closely with data scientists, analysts, and other stakeholders to ensure that the data infrastructure supports the organization's goals and requirements.
