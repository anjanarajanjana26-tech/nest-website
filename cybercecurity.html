Cyber Security

Cybercriminals constantly look for ways to exploit weaknesses in systems, networks, and personal devices. Weak passwords, outdated software, and unsecured networks give them easy access. They use advanced tactics like phishing, ransomware, and social engineering to steal data, disrupt systems, or demand money. With AI-driven threats, even bots can breach security, making cybersecurity more important than ever.
